A brief definitions of what Transhumanism:

"Transhumanism (abbreviated as H+ or h+) is an international and intellectual movement that aims to transform the human condition by developing and creating widely available sophisticated technologies to greatly enhance human intellectual, physical, and psychological capacities. Transhumanist thinkers study the potential benefits and dangers of emerging technologies that could overcome fundamental human limitations, as well as the ethics of using such technologies. The most common transhumanist thesis is that human beings may eventually be able to transform themselves into different beings with abilities so greatly expanded from the natural condition as to merit the label of posthuman beings" (source: Wikipedia).

Transhumanism falls under the umbrella concept "posthumanism", which is the philosophical analysis of forms of being after humanism. In this sense, transhumanism is a "transitional human", a human that wants to transcend.
